Deal News

Deal news extraction takes a lot of effort.

Deal news extraction is a manual process. The first step is to extract the info of a particulary deal from various primary & secondary sources, by researching on Google. The second step is to analysing all the relevant info from the article & then write the deal in client database.

Primary sources are numerous. We check the LP/GP company website, Bidder/Buyer company website, Target company website, Seller company webiste & any other PE/VC company website, if it's name is mentioned in the article.

Secondary sources have high volume. Sites like PR Newswire, Venture Capital Journal, PE Hub, Your Story, Deal Street Asia, Globallegal Chronicle, etc.

LLMs can automate this extraction.
